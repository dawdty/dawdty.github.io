- evil hand (arduino, robotics, pixel art, 2021)

made for the art, technology, and computing class at NCSSM

collaborators: jack huo

assignment: create a piece interactive art designed to make the viewer uncomfortable

meant to be experienced in a dark room. participants lift up a blanket and look through a peephole to “shake hands” with an evil digital entity on the other side. electrode detects touch and lightly grasps the subject, face slowly morphs in an unsettling way as the grip tightens.
